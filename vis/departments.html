Department Reference — UChicago Dissertation Spatialization

All 42 canonical departments · historical predecessors · building timelines · sources  |  ← Back to visualization

Purpose. This document provides full sourcing for every department in the dataset. For each of the 42 canonical modern departments it records: the current UChicago program page, every historical department name that maps to it (from the DEPT_MAP normalization table in GIS/dept_housing.py), the building history used to place dissertations on the campus map (from DEPT_TIMELINE in GIS/prepare_viz_data.py), and the sources behind any judgment calls.

URL verification. Department URLs follow standard UChicago conventions but should be verified before citation — the university occasionally reorganizes web properties. Links marked ⚠ verify are best-guess patterns; all others are high-confidence based on known UChicago web conventions.

Typos. The source data contains numerous OCR and transcription errors (e.g., "Biochemistrt", "Phsiology", "Poltical Science"). These are listed in DEPT_MAP but are not repeated here in the interest of readability; every typo variant maps to the same canonical department as its correctly-spelled counterpart.

Judgment — "Planning" and "International Relations" → Public Policy Studies: The Committee on Planning and the Committee on International Relations are early precursors to what became the Harris School. They were social science committees with strong policy and applied public administration orientations. Mapping them to Public Policy Studies reflects their functional and intellectual lineage, though no direct institutional continuity is documented. Remaining UNKNOWN Assignments

The following department strings appear in the source data and are mapped to UNKNOWN in the pipeline. In all cases this is intentional: either the program no longer exists with no clear modern successor and no confirmed building location, the record is too ambiguous to assign, or the program was on the medical campus outside the academic quad. 1,351 records total (4.2%).

Medical Campus Clinical Departments

Raw strings: Radiology, Radiological Physics, Radiation and Cellular Oncology, Ophthalmology, Ophthalmology & Visual Science
Reason for UNKNOWN: These are clinical departments of the Pritzker School of Medicine located on the medical campus (S. Maryland Ave / Hyde Park medical complex), which is outside the academic quad covered by this visualization. No equivalent PhD-granting department in the canonical 42. Surgery, Medicine, and Pathology, which sometimes appear in early records as dissertation departments, are mapped to OBA (see OBA judgment note).
Source: UChicago Pritzker School of Medicine clinical department directories.
Dataset count: ~41 records.

Division-Level Entries (too broad to assign)

Raw strings: Division of Social Sciences, Division of the Social Sciences, Division of Humanities, Division of the Humanities, Division of Physical Sciences, Division of Biological Sciences, Division of Biological Sciences and Pritzker School of Medicine
Reason for UNKNOWN: These strings indicate only which of the four academic divisions granted the degree — not which specific department the student was in. Without a department-level assignment, no spatial or topical attribution can be made.
Dataset count: several hundred records, primarily from early HathiTrust data where department was not separately recorded.

The Law School

Raw strings: The Law School
Reason for UNKNOWN: The Law School awards JD and LLM degrees, not PhD dissertations, in the standard sense. The small number of records carrying "The Law School" as department are likely SJD (Doctor of Juridical Science) records or data entry errors in the ProQuest export. No campus building in the canonical 42 corresponds to the Law School.
